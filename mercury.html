Fun Facts about Mercury

  • Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system.
  • It orbits very close to the Sun and has no moons.
  • A day on Mercury (one rotation) takes about 59 Earth days, but a year (one orbit around the Sun) is only about 88 Earth days!
  • Mercury has a very thin atmosphere, mostly made of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.
  • Temperatures on Mercury can range from -173°C at night to 427°C during the day—hot enough to melt lead!
  • Mercury's surface is covered in craters, similar to the Moon's, and has vast plains and steep cliffs.
  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun, but it's not the hottest. Venus, with its thick atmosphere, holds that title.
